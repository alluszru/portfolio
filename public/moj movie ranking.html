The Best Movies According to Urszulka

My top 3 movies off all times

Secretariat

Secretariat is a biographical sports film about a legendary racehorse who overcomes great odds to achieve extraordinary success. It follows his owner, Penny Chenery, as she leads him to victory in the Triple Crown and makes history in horse racing.

McFarland

McFarland is a sports drama based on a true story about a high school cross-country team made up of Latino students. It follows their coach as he helps them overcome personal struggles and achieve success through teamwork and determination.

Remember the Titans

Remember the Titans is a sports drama based on a true story about a newly integrated high school football team in the 1970s. The film follows Coach Boone and his players as they overcome racism, learn teamwork, and unite to achieve success on and off the field.
